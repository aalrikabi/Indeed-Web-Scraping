Senior Statistician ( (Login for information) , Charlotte, NC) Use advanced statistical and computational methodologies to deliver insights and strategic opportunities to improve the quality, patient experience, and cost of healthcare. Establish scalable, efficient, automated processes for large scale data analyses and model development, validation and implementation. Monitor and analyze complex systems to understand, diagnose, and improve key performance indicators. Assist with the design of research protocols including sample size calculations and randomization. Write statistical methodology and results for technical reports and publications. Provide data and analyses for grant preparation for outside funding. Manipulate, combine, and refine large databases to produce information suitable for analysis. Create graphics for data visualization and information display. Work with researchers to shape and direct research questions. Develop metrics and scorecards. Assist with ad hoc requests or projects. Requires a Master's degree in Statistics, Biostatistics or related with coursework in statistical methodology. Requires five (5) years progressive statistical and biostatistical experience to include five (5) years in: SAS or R statistical programming languages; and working with datasets and data manipulation. Requires one (1) publication in a scientific manuscript or conference abstract. Must submit to pre-employment background check and drug screen. Apply at (Login for information) Ref #64421.
