Business Systems Consultant 6
DIVERSANT LLC - Charlotte, NC
Our client has an immediate need for a talented Capital Markets BA that brings experience in the Finance Industry. This is a 12 month + contract opportunity with long-term potential and is located in Charlotte, NC. Please review the below job description and reply with the most current MS Word version of your resume.

Responsibilities:
Candidates will be responsible for the business analysis in technology supporting the fixed income and derivative trading businesses. In this role, the candidates must be able to translate capital markets trading business needs into technical requirements, ensuring all systems support and conform to the requirements and specifications.
The candidate must be a liaison between Front Office, Middle Office and Back Office business units and software developers. The candidate will work autonomously in a team of developers, other business analysts, and Quality Assurance testers.

Key Requirements:
  • 5+ years experience of hands on business analysis experience
  • Strong capital markets business knowledge
  • Strong working knowledge of Fixed Income, IR Derivatives, Equities, FX and Exchange Traded products.
  • Experience with Data Capture methods - acquiring and abstracting primary or secondary data from existing internal or external data sources
  • Strong working knowledge of SQL
  • Technical expertise regarding data models and database design development
  • Experience in data mining techniques and procedures and knowing when their use is appropriate
  • Strong working knowledge of Excel, VBA preferred
  • Knowledge of statistics
  • The best business analyst candidate is someone who has a technical background or has proven expertise working tightly with/on a development team.
  • We are not looking for a project coordinator or pure business analysts that have only worked for the business.
  • Knowledge of Bloomberg, PME, or other reference data platforms a plus
