SAT/ACT Tutor
Forgione Tutoring - Raleigh, NC
$25 an hour
Job Description
Forgione Tutoring is looking for enthusiastic and dynamic educators to conduct private SAT and ACT lessons to high school students in our Raleigh a nd Cary centers and in local homes. This is a part-time position with training required.

Open positions
-*A tutor with experience teaching all parts of the SAT and ACT (reading, math, and writing/grammar) at both locations
  • SAT/ACT Reading and English (mainly for Cary location)
  • SAT/ACT Math and Science (both Cary and Raleigh locations)
  • An instructor with experience teaching all parts of the SAT/ACT will be given preference.
(Note: We cannot accept applications from residents outside of Wake Forest, Raleigh, Cary, or Apex due to the geographic limits of in-home tutoring. The majority of our students live in Wake County.)

Qualifications required
2-3 years minimum teaching or tutoring experience
Bachelor's degree
An engaging and motivational teaching style that communicates an enthusiasm for learning
A passion for helping students reach their goals
Excellent communication skills and professional presentation skills
Excellent organization skills
Strong customer focus
Record of strong standardized test-taking and academic performance
Strong background in English, reading comprehension, writing, vocabulary, and grammar
--and/or--
Strong background in SAT and ACT-level mathematics: (numbers and operations; pre-algebra, advanced algebra and functions; geometry; data analysis, statistics, and probability; trigonometry)
ACT instructors only: proficiency in a variety of science subjects
Ability to pass a screening test that covers relevant subject matter
Reliable transportation
Available afternoons/evenings and at least one weekend day
A minimum of 8 hours of availability per week, to include at least one weekend day

Training
Successful completion of a training program, length of which to depend on the instructor's level of experience teaching test preparation (tutors are compensated for training after successful completion of all required training and materials). Training ranges from 10 - 25 hours depending on the number of subjects; Applicant must be able to pass a background check.

Responsibilities
  • Assist high school students in the mastery of test-taking skills, techniques, and SAT and ACT subject matter
  • Teach weekly lessons, following the company's syllabi and curriculum
  • Monitor and record student progress
  • Communicate regularly with parents about student progress and opportunities for students
  • Identify and counsel students who are not improving and use experience and judgment to assist the students in reaching their goals
  • Maintain effective and timely communication with supervisor regarding students' progress
  • Maintain administrative duties in a thorough and organized way (filling out and turning in of paperwork, such as session notes, time sheets, and other communications)
  • Participate in Forgione Tutoring's professional development workshops and staff meetings as scheduled
About Us

Forgione Tutoring LLC is a Raleigh, NC-based test preparation and academic coaching company focused on providing high-quality instruction for the SAT, ACT, and other standardized tests, as well as subject tutoring and the college application process. Heather Forgione has tutored hundreds of students since her initial training 15 years ago. Forgione Tutoring began in 2007 and has served hundreds of students since.

Instructors will have the benefit of using our Strategy Workbook and curriculum as their primary teaching source, but will also be able to supplement the lessons with their own techniques to customize lessons to each student. Instructors can set their own schedules and conduct the majority of the lessons at our two locations, with one day of the week dedicated to traveling to students within 20 minutes of the tutor's home.

As a small, family-owned company, we are committed to providing outstanding customer service as well as a supportive environment for employees.

Application Process

Visit our website at www.forgionetutoring.com and click on Jobs. Complete and email the Tutor Application, along with a resume and cover letter to the email indicated. The resume should prioritize any relevant work experience. The cover letter should include the reasons for your interest in teaching SAT and ACT, how your experience has prepared you to tutor these tests, and any other information that would help us to better understand how your skills and experience apply to this position.

Starting pay is $25/hr for any tutoring conducted during the 3-month orientation period.
Pay is increased to $30/hr upon successful completion of orientation and training
